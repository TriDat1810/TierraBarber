About Us

At Tierra Barber & Shave, every haircut tells a story of passion, skill, and experience. With over 10 years in the craft, our founder started from scratch, fueled by dedication and a love for barbering. Each cut is delivered with precision and care—because it's more than just hair, it’s your identity.

Conveniently located inside the Shell Gas Station at 52 & Santo, we proudly serve the Tierrasanta and San Diego communities with two locations. Our services include Men’s Haircuts, Fades, Beard Grooming, Straight Razor Shaves, Military Cuts, and Kids & Senior Trims.

We offer both walk-ins and free appointments to suit your schedule. At Tierra, we don’t just offer a service—we create an experience where you leave not only looking sharp but feeling confident.

“Not just a haircut, but a commitment to craftsmanship.”

FAQs

What services do you offer?
We offer haircuts, shaves, beard trims, and more.
Are your barbers experienced?
All our barbers are highly trained and have years of experience in the industry.
Do I need to book in advance?
Walk-ins are welcome, but booking is recommended for your convenience.
Can I choose my barber?
Yes, you can request a specific barber when booking your appointment.
Where are you located?
We are located at 6030 Santo Rd Ste C, San Diego, CA 92124.
What are your opening hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 09:30 to 18:30, and Sunday to 17:00.
Can I bring my child for a haircut?
Absolutely! We welcome clients of all ages, including children.
Is parking available?
Yes, there is free parking available right outside the shop.
